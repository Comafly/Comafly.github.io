FeatherDEX



FeatherDex is a gamified bird-watching app. It uses geolocation to track the user's position, and then accesses local wildlife information using the 'Encyclopedia of Life' API to create a list of the most common birds in the area.

The user is tasked with spotting birds, recording their behaviour, and taking photos to fill out their local encyclopedia, unlock badges, and improve their leaderboard ranking.

There's also an integrated social system for sharing spots and photos, and a feature-rich merch store, including shopping cart and Paypal integration.

  • API access to 'Encyclopedia of Life' animal database.
  • User profile system with progress tracking and leaderboards.
  • Integrated Friend and Social systems for sharing progress.
  • Complete e-commerce store experience with Paypal integration.
